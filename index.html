Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how MERYSOLD INITIATIVES & TECHNOLOGY (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information in connection with our mobile games (“the Apps”). We are strongly committed to protecting players and keeping our games safe and simple.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to all mobile games and applications published by MERYSOLD INITIATIVES & TECHNOLOGY, regardless of platform (such as Google Play or other app stores).

2. We Do Not Collect Any Data

Our Apps are designed to run entirely on the user’s device. We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal or non-personal data.

This means, in particular, that we do not collect:

  • Names, nicknames, or contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or approximate location)
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or other technical IDs
  • Usage statistics, analytics, or gameplay behavior
  • Payment information or purchase history
  • Any other information that could identify a user

3. No Third-Party SDKs or Tracking Technologies

Our Apps do not integrate advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social media plug-ins, or any similar third-party technologies that might collect information about you.

In other words, we do not use:

  • Advertising SDKs or ad-tracking tools
  • Analytics SDKs or crash/usage analytics
  • Push notification services linked to user identifiers
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are suitable for a wide audience, including younger players. Because we do not collect any information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under any specific age threshold (such as 13 or 16) under applicable law.

5. Local Storage on Device

Certain game elements (such as level progress, scores, unlocked items) may be stored locally on the user’s device only. This information never leaves the device and is not accessible to us.

6. Data Security

Since we do not operate any servers or databases for user data, there is no central place where personal information is stored. Game data saved locally on the device is protected by the device’s own security mechanisms and operating system.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if our Apps change or if legal requirements are updated. The “Last Updated” date at the top of the Policy will reflect the latest version.

If we ever decide in the future to collect or process data (for example, to add ads or analytics), we will update this Policy in advance and ensure that any required notices and consents are properly implemented.
